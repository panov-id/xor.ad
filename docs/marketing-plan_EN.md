# Offline Advertising Plan (Draft)

## Purpose

Record the plan for pre-launch offline advertising — a way to test demand before full development (see also the waitlist landing pages in `sosed.place/landing` and `neighbro.place/landing`).

## Formats

- Stickers.
- Posters.
- Banners.
- Flyers.

Different designs for different materials/locations, not one universal layout.

## Placement

Around the city, in various locations where people gather — specific spots and city/cities are not chosen yet.

## Link to the waitlist

Materials lead to the corresponding face's waitlist landing (sosed.place/neighbro.place) — likely via a QR code or short link. Conversion from offline ads to the waitlist is the same signal discussed earlier: if people follow through and leave an email, there's demand; if not, the concept needs rethinking.

## Open questions

- City/cities and specific locations are not chosen yet.
- Design layouts have not been created yet.
- The way to track which specific material drove a visit to the landing (a unique QR/UTM per layout) is not defined yet.
- Budget and print run are not defined yet.
